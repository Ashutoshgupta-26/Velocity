About Us

We are NITK Velocity Racing, a dynamic team of passionate students from NITK Surathkal. Since 2010, our mission has been to design, build, and race performance bikes that push the limits of engineering and speed. With over 15 years of racing legacy, we continue to innovate and compete in events across India and abroad. Our motto, "Ride Beyond Limits," keeps us driven toward excellence.

Our Achievements

  • Winner- Formula Bharat 2023
  • Best Innovation Award - FS Germany 2022
  • Overall 2nd - Formula Student UK 2021
  • Fastest Lap Time - FS Italy 2020
  • Best Design Presentation - FS Austria 2019

Upcoming Projects

  • Project ThunderVolt-June '25- Designing and prototyping our first all-electric racing bike, combining performance and sustainability for future-ready racing.

  • ViperX Engine 3.0-July '25- In-house development of a lightweight high-performance engine optimized for track agility and endurance.

  • Advanced Aero Kit-Sep '25- Computational and wind tunnel testing to develop a race-tuned aerodynamic package, reducing drag and improving lap times.

  • Data Acquisition System-Oct '25- Development of a real-time telemetry system to collect race data, enhancing on-track decision making and post-race analysis.
